Dowód persystencji

Dodanie produktu:
curl -X POST http://localhost/api/items `
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" `
  -d '{"name":"Laptop","price":4500}'

Invoke-RestMethod `
  -Uri http://localhost/api/items `
  -Method POST `
  -ContentType "application/json" `
  -Body '{"name":"Laptop","category":"Electronics","price":4500}'

Sprawdzenie danych
curl http://localhost/api/items | ConvertTo-Json -Depth 5

Invoke-RestMethod http://localhost/api/items | ConvertTo-Json -Depth 5
